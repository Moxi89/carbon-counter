5-Minute Carbon Hacks: Quick Wins for Busy People

4 min read January 19, 2025

No time for lengthy eco-projects? No problem! These super-quick carbon hacks take just minutes to implement but can make a real difference to your carbon footprint. Perfect for busy schedules!

5-Minute Wins:
  • ✓ Power strip setup: 2 minutes
  • ✓ Water heater adjustment: 3 minutes
  • ✓ Phone charger timer: 1 minute
  • ✓ Light switch labels: 4 minutes

1. The Power Strip Setup

Take 2 minutes to plug your entertainment devices into one power strip. One switch turns everything off - no more standby power waste!

Do This Now:
  • Gather devices that stay on standby
  • Plug into one power strip
  • Place strip in easy reach
  • Switch off when not in use

Saves up to $100 per year on phantom power!

2. Water Heater Quick-Fix

Most water heaters are set too high by default. A 3-minute adjustment can save energy and still give you perfect hot water.

Perfect Settings:
  • Ideal temp: 50°C (122°F)
  • Safe for all uses
  • Saves 10% on water heating
  • Extends heater life

3. Smart Charging Setup

Phone chargers waste power when left plugged in. Take 1 minute to set up a charging routine that saves energy.

Quick Tip:

Set a phone alarm for when charging is done (usually 2 hours max). Unplug both phone and charger when done.

4. Light Switch Labels

Spend 4 minutes labeling light switches. Clear labels mean lights get turned off more often, especially in shared spaces.

Label Ideas:

Use fun reminders like "Hit me when you quit me" or "Lights out = Carbon down" to encourage everyone to switch off.

5. The 2-Minute Device Audit

Walk around your space with a timer set for 2 minutes. Note every device that's on but not being used.

Common Finds:
  • Printers in standby
  • Unused chargers
  • TV boxes on standby
  • Kitchen appliances displaying time

What's Next?

Pick just one of these hacks to try right now. Each takes less time than brewing a cup of coffee but saves energy all year long.

Your First Step:

Start with the power strip hack - it's the quickest win with the biggest impact. Set a 2-minute timer and go!
